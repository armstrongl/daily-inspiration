Each day, this site publishes three short messages drawn from substantive primary sources: a cognitive jolt (something that disrupts a default assumption), an emotional lift (something that evokes genuine wonder or scale), and an action prompt (a concrete practice grounded in a real exemplar).

Every claim links to its source. Sources are filtered against pseudo-profundity, variety is enforced across domains and source types, and nothing repeats within a 60-day window.
