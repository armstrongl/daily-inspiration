# Inspiration log

This file tracks every source surfaced by the daily-inspiration skill. Used to enforce the 60-day non-repetition window and to check variety in domains, source types, and registers.

## Log format

| Date | Slot | Domain | Source type | Source | Summary | Score |
|------|------|--------|-------------|--------|---------|-------|

## Notes

- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD format.
- **Slot:** One of `jolt`, `lift`, `prompt`, or `NOT FILLED`.
- **Domain:** The primary knowledge domain (e.g., neuroscience, history, mathematics, ecology).
- **Source type:** One of: research paper, essay, book excerpt, historical document, conference talk, technical report, practitioner account, raindrop bookmark, reframe.
- **Source:** Title or URL of the primary source.
- **Summary:** One line capturing the specific content surfaced (enough to recognize if it's being repeated).
- **Score:** The Tier 2 + Tier 3 composite score (0-8).
